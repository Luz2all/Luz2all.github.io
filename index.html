2048

A faithful single-file clone of the classic 2048 sliding-tile puzzle. Merge tiles with the same number to reach 2048. Controls: arrow keys or swipe.

URL: /2048.html

Water Bottle Flip

A physics-based bottle flip game. Hold to charge a flip and release to try to land the bottle upright. Swipe and touch controls supported. Progress (attempts/successes) is stored locally.

URL: /waterbottle/
